Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection

We only collect essential data to provide moderation services. This includes Discord User IDs to track warnings (warns), active mutes, and temporary bans. We do not collect personal names, emails, or real-life locations.

2. Data Usage

The collected data is used exclusively for the bot's internal functions. Your data is never used for advertising, marketing, or tracking outside of the Discord environment.

3. Data Retention

Logs are stored on our secure servers hosted by FPS.ms. Data is deleted automatically once a penalty (like a ban) expires, or upon manual removal by a server administrator.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to request the deletion of your data. To do so, please contact the bot owner via our support channels.
